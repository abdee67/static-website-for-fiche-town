    • SELALE UNIVERSITY

    Selale University is a newly established public university located in Ethiopia specifically situated at “Fiche” town. It is the administrative centre of the Semien Shewa Zone of Oromiya Region and separate woreda.
    Selale University was established in July 2016 as per the Proclamation article/no.359/2008.

    Location

    The university is located at Fitche, 120km away from the capital Addis Ababa, in North Shewa Zone of Oromia Regional State, Ethiopia.


    Campuses

    The university has three campuses viz. General Tadesse Birru (main) Campus, AbebechGobena (Health Science) Campus and Ganda Farda Campus which is under construction.


    Admission

    The admission into regular undergraduate courses is done through based on results of the Ethiopian Higher Education Entrance Certificate Examination (EHEECE), the choice of the student and the capacity of Departments/Schools/Centers.


       

    course offered

    With its 201 academic and 310 administrative staff, the university runs 19 programs/departments under five colleges.
    There are 422 and 845 students in regular and extension programs, respectively.
